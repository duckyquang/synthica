Synthica Competition

Showcase your research on a global stage and compete with the best high school researchers worldwide.

Annual Competition

Celebrate Research Excellence

The Synthica Competition is an annual event that brings together passionate high school researchers from around the world to present their findings, receive feedback from experts, and compete for recognition and prizes.

Timeline

Competition Timeline

Submission Deadline

March - April

Review Period

Expert judges review all submissions and select regional finalists.

May

Regional Rounds

June

International Round

Top researchers from each region compete in the international finals.

July

Awards Ceremony

Evaluation Criteria

Judging Rubric

Research submissions are evaluated based on the following criteria by our panel of expert judges from top universities.

30%

Research Quality

  • Originality of research question
  • Appropriate methodology
  • Validity of results
  • Depth of analysis
25%

Impact & Significance

  • Contribution to the field
  • Real-world applications
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Future research potential
20%

Written Communication

  • Clarity of writing
  • Logical organization
  • Proper citations
  • Grammar and style
15%

Presentation Skills

  • Clear delivery
  • Effective visuals
  • Time management
  • Engagement
10%

Q&A Performance

  • Understanding of topic
  • Response clarity
  • Critical thinking
  • Professional conduct
