Want to know how we work?
Here's how.

Learn how Synthica works and become a part of us.

1

Apply to become a Chapter Leader

Go through the processing of applying to become a Chapter Leader at your school by working our regional ambassador team.

2

Open a Synthica Chapter in your school

Set up a Synthica Chapter at your school by creating a research club and begin recruiting members to join your club.

3

Teach our prepared curriculum to members

Create slides based on our provided curriculum and teach your members about how to conduct a research project.

4

Prepare your teams for the Synthica Competition

Begin to divide your members into teams and brainstorm on your research ideas to propose to the regional round.

5
